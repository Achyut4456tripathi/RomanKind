Get Found on Google: A Simple SEO Guide

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of getting "Free" traffic from Google. If you want to earn money from AdSense, you need SEO.

3 SEO Essentials:

  1. Keywords: Use words people actually search for in your titles.
  2. Mobile Friendly: Ensure your site looks great on a smartphone.
  3. Backlinks: Get other websites to link to your tutorials.

Conclusion: SEO takes time, but it is the most consistent way to grow your business.
